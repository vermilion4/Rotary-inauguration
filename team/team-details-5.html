Rotn. Arinze Okamelu

Secretary

Arinze Okamelu is a Media and Marketing Communication expert with bias in Public Relations, Marketing and Advertising.

He is a Rotarian over 25 years, a member and Past President of the Rotary Club of Oregun and currently an Assistant Governor in Rotary International District 9110,Nigeria.

He has chaired several Rotary committees, both at his club and at district level, and he is currently the Secretary of the 2023 District Governor Installation Committee.

Rotn. Arinze as he is fondly called, is married with children.
