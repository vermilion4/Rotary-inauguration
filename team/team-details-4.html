DGE Ifeyinwa Ejezie

Ifeyinwa Rita Ejezie is an accomplished professional and District Governor for Rotary Year 2023-24. She has a strong commitment to the Rotary Foundation and has made significant personal contributions. With a background in Rotaract and years of experience in Rotary, she has held various high-ranking positions within District 9110, showcasing her dedication to service and leadership.

In addition to her Rotary involvement, Ify has made notable contributions to Nigeria's financial and capital market industry. She has served in key roles in organizations such as the Association of Stockbroking Houses, where she was the Public Relations Officer and a member of the Executive Governing Council. Her expertise was instrumental in the implementation of the Automated Trading System on the Nigerian Stock Exchange, marking a significant advancement in trading practices.

Ify's educational background includes prestigious degrees in corporate governance, finance, banking, and business administration. She holds an MSC in Corporate Governance & Finance, an MBA in Banking & Finance, and a Higher National Diploma in Business Administration, among other qualifications. Alongside her professional achievements, Ify is a loving mother and an active member of the Ikoyi Club 1938. Her leisure activities include traveling, meeting new people, engaging in creative discussions, reading for knowledge, and participating in humanitarian efforts.
