Event Details

Dear Rotarians, Let's get ready for an experience like no other as we celebrate a historic event in Rotary District 9110! On July 15th, 2023, DGE Ifenyinwa Ejezie will be installed as the 43rd District Governor and the 2nd Female District Governor of our dear district. The Grand Installation Ceremony will take place at the luxurious Grand Ballroom of the Oriental Hotel Lekki, and it's sure to be a day to remember. Join us for a celebration filled with excitement and inspiration. So, mark your calendars, save the date, and secure your tickets early! You have not seen anything like it before. Stay Tuned!
