AG. Sunit Deb Roy

Vice Chairman

Dr Sunit Deb Roy, a Structural Engineer has more than 22 Years of Experience in Construction, Construction Management, Civil Engineering Designs & Contract Management. As Business Head for the Construction Division of Purechem Manufacturing Limited, he drove the construction business to a new dimension.

Dr Sunit Deb Roy, also comes with experience in Construction Chemicals, Design & Execution of Bridges and Tunnel Projects.

Dr Sunit Deb Roy, has working experience in Nigeria for more than a decade. He is the charter president of Rotary club of Eko Atlantic, an Assistant Governor, Rotary International District 9110 and a major donor to Rotary Foundation.
