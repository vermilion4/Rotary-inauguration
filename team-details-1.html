PAG. Okechukwu UCHE-UKAH

Chairman

Rotarian okechukwu UCHE-UKAH is the Chairman of 2023 District Governor Installation Committee; a member and Past President of Rotary Club of Ojodu, and currently a Past Assistant Governor, in Rotary District 9110, Nigeria, having supervised several clubs on behalf of three different District Governors.

He is the Chairman and Group Managing Director of Mit-Glauben Engineering Group, and Chairman, WeRblack Winery Group representing KWV South Africa; among other business interests.

Rotarian okechukwu UCHE-UKAH is a major donor to the Rotary foundation.
